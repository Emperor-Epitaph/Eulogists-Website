Who are we?

Emperor Epitaph

One of the founding members of “The Eulogists”. He started playing piano when he was 10 and has passed many of his music exams. He later saw interest of playing guitar when he was 16 but wasn’t fully devoted until he was 18. Upon learning the guitar he also picked up Bass guitar which interested him even more. Now he’s currently running a personal YouTube channel and became apart of “The Eulogists”.

Quinn Bellmore

Another founding member of The Eulogists, Quinn has been playing piano since he was 5. He has interests in music development software, and enjoys programming. He's passed all of his piano exams, and theory exams, and will soon be graduating from the Royal Conservatory of Music piano course.

Taliyah Harris

Born into a life full of music, Taliyah has been a performer for as long as she can remember. From singing and dancing as a child to playing a bunch of instruments in her latter school years. Over time, she went from performing in a group to being in the spotlight by her senior year. Now she writes her own music, is the newest member of the Eulogists, and is excited to go to college to preserve the magic of the music as she studies music education.
