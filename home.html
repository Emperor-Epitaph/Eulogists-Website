History of The Eulogists

2
March

The Birth of The Eulogists

This day was the day that the The Eulogists were formed starting with Calvin Liu, and Quinn Bellmore later known as Emperor Epitaph and HI-FI Addict

3
March

RNCMusic joins the group

Not long afterwards Rylan was invited to join The Eulogists and as of now is still our Spokesman

15
March

The Eulogists first video

The Eulogists first video which was King Crimson - Epitaph Cover (ft. Emperor Epitaph) gained a positive perception to audiences

17
March

First Eulogist Topic video

The Psychology of Music (ft. RNC Music) was The Eulogists first informative video it hit over 100 views which gave The Eulogists to continue working on Musical Topics.

First Mashup

The Eulogist's first mashup video was a hit it reached over 389 views which gave them a better reason to continue on making types of musical videos.
